Available Test Categories

Hematology

Blood cell counts & clotting studies to detect anemia, infections, and bleeding disorders.

Common tests
  • CBC (Complete Blood Count), ESR
  • Peripheral Smear, Reticulocyte Count
  • PT/INR, APTT (Coagulation Profile)

Clinical Biochemistry

Organ function & metabolic health using blood and body fluid analytes.

Common tests
  • Glucose (Fasting, PP), HbA1c
  • Lipid Profile, LFT, RFT
  • Electrolytes (Na, K, Cl, Ca, Mg, Phos)
  • Thyroid profile (T3, T4, TSH)
  • Cardiac markers (Troponin, CK‑MB, LDH)

Immunoassay & Hormones

Hormones, vitamins, and tumor markers for endocrine and cancer care.

Common tests
  • FSH, LH, Prolactin, Testosterone, Estradiol, Progesterone

Clinical Pathology

Urine, stool & body fluid analysis to evaluate infection and organ disease.

Common tests
  • Urine Routine & Microscopy

  • CSF, Pleural, Ascitic, Synovial fluids
  • Semen Analysis

Microbiology & Serology

Cultures, rapid tests & serology for bacterial, viral and fungal infections.

FAQs

How do I prepare for fasting blood tests?

Typically 8–12 hours of fasting (water allowed). Follow your doctor’s advice for medications.

How quickly will I receive my report?

Most routine tests are reported the same day or next morning. Specialized tests may take longer.

Do you provide home sample collection?

Yes. Call our desk or book online to schedule a convenient home visit.
